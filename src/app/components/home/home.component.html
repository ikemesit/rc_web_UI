Start your Rosicrucian Journey

We care about your spiritual growth and well-being…

Our mission is to assist all seekers of truth discover spiritual wisdom that enables them to live in harmony with the creative, constructive cosmic forces for the attainment of health, happiness and peace.

We teach our students timeless, universal Rosicrucian laws and principles and practical methods and techniques of application to address life’s problems.

…and The Awakening of your Psychic consciousness

The sustained study, investigation and practical application of these laws and principles enhances the spiritual development of the student, deepens understanding of the self, builds self-confidence that leads to self-mastery and illumination.

We nurture our students to be active global citizens, dedicated to the happiness and welfare of humanity by demonstrating a spirit of service, brotherhood, understanding, cooperation, tolerance, and goodwill to all.
